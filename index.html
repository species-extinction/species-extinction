This page aims to show people the situation of the extincted species around the world.
It contains three main parts:
  • World Map:
    The details of the extincted spicies around the world, in which you can select a country and select the informations that you are interested in.
    You can choose to see:
    • Causes: The proportion of extinction causes of extinct species in this region.
    • Habitats: The statistics of the living environment of extinct species in this region.
    • List: The list of extincted species.
    • Classification: The classification of the extinct species by their phylum in the selected region.
  • Overview: The evolution of the number of species extincted over time by causes of extinction.
  • Observations: Some observations we made from the graphs in the previous sections

Select a country to get more information.

In Australia, there were 41 extinctions, in Tanzania, Uganda and Kenya there were around 40 extinctions and in Mexico 29.
Most other country had less than 10 extinctions or we don't have data on them.
